About us

A church community shaped by grace and grounded in scripture.

Oke Ibukun Rehoboth exists to help people encounter Jesus, grow in faith, and carry hope into everyday life. This frontend lays out the digital foundation for that experience.

Our mission

We gather people into authentic worship, equip believers through clear biblical teaching, and send them out to love their city well.

Our vision

To build a Christ-centered, spiritually healthy, and outward-facing church that welcomes every generation into meaningful discipleship.

Our values

Prayer, scripture, compassion, excellence, and community shape how we serve, communicate, and steward every ministry expression.

What to expect

Warm people, heartfelt worship, and practical teaching.

Whether someone is new to church or has served for years, they should feel welcomed, informed, and able to engage with the life of the church both online and in person.
